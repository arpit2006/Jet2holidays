My Trip to the Majestic Hundru Falls!

Just visited Hundru Falls and was blown away by the sheer power of the water. It's the highest waterfall in the state and a must-see. The 750 steps down are a bit of a workout, but the view from the bottom is totally worth it. Pro-tip: Go after the monsoon season for the best flow!

The climb to Parasnath Hills (Shikharji)

Completed the pilgrimage to Shikharji. It's a challenging 27 km trek (up and down), but the sense of peace at the top is unparalleled. It's the most important Jain tirtha. My advice: start very early in the morning (around 3 AM) to avoid the heat.
