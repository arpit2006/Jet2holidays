Dassam Falls

Dassam Falls, located near Ranchi in Jharkhand, is a spectacular waterfall dropping from a height of about 144 feet. Surrounded by dense forests and rocky terrain, it is a popular destination for travelers seeking natural beauty and peace. The sound of the gushing water and the refreshing atmosphere make it a perfect spot for relaxation and photography.

Visitors often enjoy picnics and nature walks around the falls, but swimming is not recommended due to strong currents. Easily accessible from Ranchi, Dassam Falls is a must-visit for anyone exploring the scenic wonders of Jharkhand.

Trikuta Parvata

Trikuta Parvata, located near Deoghar in Jharkhand, is a sacred hill with three prominent peaks. It is deeply revered in Hindu mythology and is associated with Lord Shiva, making it a significant pilgrimage site.
Visitors can trek up the hill to enjoy panoramic views of the surrounding landscape while also experiencing the spiritual aura of this holy place.

The hill is surrounded by scenic beauty and peaceful surroundings, making it not only a destination for devotees but also for nature lovers and trekkers seeking adventure and tranquility.

Parasnath Hills

Rising to 1,365 meters, Parasnath Hills is the highest peak in Jharkhand and one of the holiest pilgrimage sites for the Jain community. Surrounded by dense forests and scenic trails, the hill offers breathtaking views along with spiritual serenity.
Trekking to the summit is a popular activity, where visitors can explore ancient Jain temples and enjoy the peaceful natural surroundings.

The best time to visit Parasnath Hills is between October and March when the weather is pleasant, making it ideal for both pilgrims and adventure seekers looking to experience spirituality and nature together.

Lodh Falls

Lodh Falls, also known as Budha Ghagh, is the highest waterfall in Jharkhand, plunging nearly 469 feet amidst the dense forests of Latehar. The thunderous roar of the water can be heard from miles away, making it one of the most spectacular natural wonders of the state.
Surrounded by rich greenery and rocky terrain, the falls create a breathtaking view that attracts nature lovers, trekkers, and photographers alike.
